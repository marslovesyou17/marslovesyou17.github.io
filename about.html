~ From Mars Themself ~

Hi! I'm Mars. I have the BIGGEST passion for music making. I'm not as passionate for anything else in my life. My genre always shifts, which is why I label my music "Alternative" for the most part. I love to experiment with sounds. My music can mainly be described as pop, but I also have folksy, acoustic, rock, alternative, alt-pop, and indie songs. I write a lot about sad stuff, but I do it because without a conflict, a story is not a story. Without negatives, there is no reason to be positive.
